### Step 2: Name Your Digital Pet (2 minutes)

**Mission:** Create a VM with a memorable name

**Steps:**
1. **Click:** "+ Create" ‚Üí "Virtual machine"
2. **Choose:** Your Azure subscription (probably says "Free Trial")
3. **Resource Group:** Create new ‚Üí Name it "my-first-vm-rg"
4. **VM Name:** Pick something fun! Examples:
   - MyFirstPet
   - AzureRocket
   - CloudBeast
   - LearningMachine

**Fun Naming Ideas:**
- Use your favorite superhero name
- Combine your pet's name + "Azure"
- Pick a character from your favorite game
- Use "Learning" + random cool word

**ADHD Tip:** Don't overthink it! You can always delete this VM later.

### Step 3: Choose Your Pet's Size (2 minutes)

**Mission:** Pick the right "food" for your digital pet

**Recommended Choice:**
- **Size:** B1s (1 CPU, 1 GB RAM) - Perfect for learning
- **Operating System:** Windows Server 2022 (familiar if you use Windows)
- **Alternative:** Ubuntu 20.04 LTS (if you want to try Linux)

**Why B1s?**
- ‚úÖ **Cheap:** Costs pennies per hour
- ‚úÖ **Fast:** Boots up quickly  
- ‚úÖ **Perfect for Learning:** Handles basic tasks easily
- ‚úÖ **ADHD-Friendly:** Less overwhelming than bigger options

### Step 4: Security Setup (2 minutes)

**Mission:** Create a username and password

**Username:** Pick something you'll remember (avoid "admin" or "user")
**Password:** Use something secure but memorable

**ADHD Security Hack:**
- Use a pattern: Capital + your birth year + special character + word
- Example: "Cloud2024!Learning"
- Write it down immediately (you'll need it to connect!)

**Networking:** Leave everything default for now (we'll explore later)

### Step 5: Deploy & Wait (3 minutes)

**Mission:** Hit the big red button and watch magic happen!

1. **Click:** "Review + create"
2. **Click:** "Create" (the blue button)
3. **Watch:** Deployment progress bar (satisfying for ADHD brains!)

**What's Happening Behind the Scenes:**
- Azure is finding a physical computer somewhere
- Installing your chosen operating system  
- Setting up networking and security
- Getting everything ready for you to connect

**Deployment Time:** Usually 2-4 minutes (perfect coffee break!)
